## Key differentiators (quick)
- AI Agent: 27+ specialized tools for family task planning
- Voice Capture: Whisper AI transcription, >95% accuracy
- Photo/OCR Capture: Scan handwritten lists, school flyers, receipts
- Calendar Sync: Two-way Google + Apple Calendar (15-min intervals)
- Multi-Family: Unlimited family groups, cross-household coordination
- Real-time Sync: <50ms WebSocket collaboration
- Knowledge Graph: 80% cache hit rate, <500ms cached responses
- Offline-First: Works without internet, syncs when reconnected
- Cross-Platform: iOS, Android, Web (40%+ mixed-device households)
- Natural Language: Tell the app what you need in plain English
