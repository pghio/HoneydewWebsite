## Why AI Matters for Family Vacation Planning

Traditional trip planning takes **6–12 hours** for a typical family vacation:
- Researching destinations
- Building itinerary
- Creating packing lists (per person)

**AI trip planners reduce this to 30–60 minutes** by:
- Generating complete packing lists from a simple prompt
- Suggesting itineraries based on your family's interests

- Coordinating logistics across all travelers

## FAQ

### What's the best free family vacation planner app?
Honeydew's free tier offers AI packing lists and family coordination. Wanderlog's free tier offers itinerary building. Both are strong options.

### Can AI actually plan a family vacation?
Yes, for the prep work. AI excels at generating packing lists, suggesting activities, and organizing logistics. You still book and make final decisions.

### What happened to Google Trips?
Google discontinued Google Trips in 2019. Some features were absorbed into Google Maps and Google Travel, but the dedicated family trip planning experience no longer exists.

### How do I share trip plans with my family?
Most apps support sharing: Honeydew has family groups, Wanderlog has trip collaboration, TripIt has shared itineraries. Choose based on what you're sharing (packing lists vs itineraries vs both).

### Which app works best with kids?
Honeydew is the only one with kid-specific features (per-person packing lists that account for children's needs, age-appropriate activity suggestions).
