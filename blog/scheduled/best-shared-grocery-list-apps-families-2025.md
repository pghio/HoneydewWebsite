## Tips for Shared Grocery List Success

### 1. Add When You Think of It

Don't wait. The moment you:
- Finish something
- Notice running low
- Think "we need..."

**Voice-add immediately.** It's 3 seconds.

### 2. One List for One Store

Separate lists:
- Grocery store
- Target/Walmart
- Costco
- Hardware store

Don't mix unless shopping all at once.

### 3. Include Details When Needed

Not just "milk" but:
- "Milk - 2% gallon"
- "Bread - whole wheat"
- "Cheese - sharp cheddar block"

Prevents wrong-item frustration.

### 4. Check Off Immediately

When item goes in cart ‚Üí Check it off.
Partner can see you got it.
No "did you get my item?" texts.

### 5. Review Before Shopping

Quick scan before leaving:
- Anything missing?
- Anything already have?
- Any duplicates?

30 seconds prevents wasted trips.
