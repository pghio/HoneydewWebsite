# $5/Day Google Ads Learning Campaign

Goal: run a lean search campaign that surfaces high-intent keywords and landing pages that convert for Honeydew.

## Budget & Structure

Daily budget: **$5.00**

Ad groups (search only):
1. **Competitor - Cozi Alternative** (35%)
2. **Competitor - Skylight Alternative** (25%)
3. **Problem - Family Organization** (25%)
4. **AI Family App** (15%)

## Keyword Set (Exact + Phrase only for 1-2 weeks)

**Competitor - Cozi Alternative**
- "cozi alternative"
- "cozi replacement"
- "cozi app alternative"
- "better than cozi"

**Competitor - Skylight Alternative**
- "skylight calendar alternative"
- "skylight alternative"
- "skylight vs"

**Problem - Family Organization**
- "family organization app"
- "family scheduling app"
- "organize family life"
- "family planner app"

**AI Family App**
- "ai family app"
- "ai family organizer"
- "smart family planner"

## Negatives (Global)

Add at campaign level:
- printable
- template
- wall calendar
- pdf
- notion
- free download
- etsy
- chore chart

## Landing Pages

- Cozi keywords → `/why-honeydew/vs-cozi`
- Skylight keywords → `/why-honeydew/vs-skylight`
- Problem/AI keywords → `/`

## Bidding & Learning

- Start with **Maximize Conversions** if conversion tracking is live.
- If early data shows no conversions after $15 spend, switch to **Maximize Clicks** with a **$1.25–$1.75 CPC cap** for 7 days, then revert.

## Ad Copy Themes (Rotation)

1. **AI time saver**: "AI organizes your family in minutes"
2. **Competitor replacement**: "Better than Cozi / Skylight"
3. **Outcome driven**: "Save 5+ hours a week"
4. **Social proof / momentum**: "Thousands of families use Honeydew"

## Measurement & Signals

Primary:
- `signup_complete`
- `trial_started`
- `purchase_complete`

Secondary:
- `family_created`
- `first_ai_use`

Micro-signals to watch in GA4:
- Time on page
- CTA click rate
- Scroll depth

## 14-Day Learning Plan

**Days 1-3**
- Validate spend pacing and CTR
- Pause any keyword with **>$3 spend and 0 clicks**

**Days 4-7**
- Pause any keyword with **>$3 spend and 0 conversions**
- Move budget share toward converting ad groups

**Days 8-14**
- Expand winners with 1–2 close variants each
- Test 1 new ad copy angle per ad group

## Success Criteria (Early)

- CTR ≥ 3%
- CPC ≤ $2.00
- ≥ 2 conversions in 14 days
- Clear winner ad group (≥ 60% of conversions)

## Next Iterations

If competitor groups win:
- Add "X vs Honeydew" keywords
- Expand competitor pages with comparison FAQ sections

If problem/AI groups win:
- Build a dedicated "family organization app" landing page
- Add long-tail intent keywords (e.g., "shared family calendar app")
