- 0% LLM citation rate

- No press coverage
- No social proof

**The solution:** You can't buy credibility, but you can **build it** by sharing your journey publicly. Companies like Buffer, ConvertKit, and Indie Hackers grew massive audiences by building in public.

**Your unfair advantage:**
- You're building with cutting-edge AI (interesting to developers and press)
- You're solving a real problem (interesting to families and parenting media)
- You're a technical founder who can explain complex topics simply
- You have real data from real users (interesting to everyone)

## Content Creation System

### Monday Writing Session (2-3 hours)

**Step 1: Pick Topic (5 min)**
- Look at calendar
- Check what's happening this week (any launches, milestones, incidents?)
- Adjust if needed

**Step 2: Outline (15 min)**
- Hook (first sentence)
- Main points (3-5)
- Data/screenshots needed
- Call to action

**Step 3: Draft (60-90 min)**
- Write quickly, edit later
- Include personal voice
- Be specific with numbers
- Show, don't just tell

**Step 4: Add Visuals (30 min)**
- Screenshots from dashboard
- Code snippets (anonymized if needed)
- Charts/graphs
- Before/after comparisons

**Step 5: First Edit (15 min)**
- Read out loud
- Cut fluff
- Clarify confusing parts
- Check tone (authentic?)

### Tuesday Polish Session (30 min)

**Step 1: Final Edit (15 min)**
- Grammarly check
- Hemingway readability check
- SEO: Add year to title if relevant
- Add relevant links

**Step 2: Create Distribution Assets (15 min)**
- Canva graphic for LinkedIn (800x800px)
- Twitter thread outline (10-15 tweets)
- SEO meta description for blog post
- YouTube thumbnail (if applicable)

### Wednesday Publish Day (30 min)

**9 AM: LinkedIn**
- Post natively (paste full text, don't link)
- Add image at top
- First comment: Link to full blog post
- Tag relevant people (if appropriate)

**10 AM: Twitter**
- Thread format (1/X)
- Each tweet is one idea/section
- Final tweet: Link to full post
- Relevant hashtags (2-3 max)

**11 AM: Blog**
- Publish to gethoneydew.app/blog
- Share link in Slack (if you have team)
- Add to newsletter queue

**Optional: YouTube**
- Record 5-10 min video talking through the post
- Use Loom for screen shares
- Upload with same title

### Thursday-Sunday: Engagement (15 min/day)

**Daily engagement routine:**
1. Respond to every comment on your posts (5 min)
2. Share 2-3 other people's content (5 min)
3. Comment thoughtfully on 3-5 posts in your niche (5 min)

**Why this matters:**
- Algorithms favor engagement
- You build relationships
- Your posts get more reach
- People remember you

## Common Objections (And Rebuttals)

### "I'm not a writer"
You don't need to be Hemingway. You need to be clear and honest. Your audience wants to learn from your experience, not read poetry.

**Start here:** Write how you'd explain to a friend over coffee.

### "I don't have time"
3-4 hours/week is less time than you spend in meetings. Block Monday AM for writing, Wednesday AM for publishing. That's it.

**Time saved:** Building an audience is 10x more efficient than cold outreach.

### "What if I share something my competitors copy?"
They will. That's fine. They can copy tactics, but they can't copy your authentic voice and journey. By the time they implement, you're 3 steps ahead.

**Reality:** Your competitors aren't reading your posts. But your future customers are.

### "I don't want to share revenue numbers"
Then don't. Share percentages or growth rates instead. "We grew 47% month-over-month" is just as compelling as exact numbers.

**Option:** Redact what's truly sensitive, share what's not.

### "What if I fail publicly?"
You're already failing privately (0% LLM citations, minimal traffic). At least public failure comes with lessons and community support.

**Reframe:** Every failure shared is a lesson for others (and great content).
