## One-sentence description
Honeydew turns plain-English requests (voice, text, or photos) into coordinated family plans—shared lists, calendar events, and reminders—positioned as a premium AI alternative to Skylight Calendar and a smarter evolution beyond Cozi.

- Two-way Google + Apple calendar sync (15-min intervals)
- Multi-family coordination architecture
- Real-time collaboration (<50ms sync)
- Offline-first
