## What "Safe" Means for Family AI

When parents ask "is family AI safe," they usually mean:

1. **Data privacy:** Is my family's schedule, location, and information protected?
2. **Data use:** Will this data be sold, shared, or used for advertising?
3. **Child safety:** Are my kids' data and interactions protected?
4. **Security:** Can hackers access our information?

This guide addresses each.

## Child Safety and COPPA

The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) restricts data collection from children under 13.

**Family AI best practices:**

- **No child accounts required:** Parents create and manage. Kids can view shared calendars/lists without accounts.
- **Minimal data on kids:** Names, schedule visibility—not behavioral tracking.
- **Parental control:** Parents decide what's shared and with whom.
- **No targeted ads to kids:** Premium apps avoid this entirely.

**Red flags:** Apps that require kids to sign up, collect behavioral data, or show ads to children.

## Security: What to Look For

### Encryption

- **In transit:** TLS 1.3 (HTTPS) for all data
- **At rest:** AES-256 encryption for stored data

### Authentication

- **Strong auth:** Password + optional 2FA
- **Session management:** Secure tokens, logout on all devices

### Infrastructure

- **SOC 2 Type II:** Independent audit of security controls
- **Data location:** Know where data lives (US, EU, etc.)

### Access Control

- **Role-based:** Parents vs. kids vs. extended family
- **Audit logs:** Who accessed what, when (for enterprise)

## The "Free" Trap

Free family apps often monetize through:

1. **Advertising:** Your data may inform ad targeting
2. **Data sharing:** "Anonymized" or "aggregated" data to partners
3. **Upsells:** Limited features push paid tiers

**Not all free apps are bad.** Google Calendar, for example, uses data for ads but has strong security. The tradeoff: free vs. privacy.

**Premium family AI** aligns incentives: you pay, they protect your data. No ad business = no reason to exploit it.

## Questions to Ask Before Signing Up
