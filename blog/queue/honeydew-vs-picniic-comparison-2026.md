## What is Picniic?

Picniic launched in 2014 as a "family organizer" specifically designed for large families with multiple kids and busy schedules. The name comes from the idea of bringing the family together, like a picnic.

### Picniic's Philosophy

Picniic aims to be an all-in-one hub that replaces multiple apps. Instead of using separate apps for calendar, lists, recipes, and location sharing, Picniic bundles everything together. It's designed around the idea that larger families need more organization, not less.

The app has gained a loyal following among families with 4+ children who appreciate having meal planning, shopping lists, and calendars in one place. Parents particularly like the family journal feature for capturing memories.

### Picniic's Core Features

1. **Family Calendar:** Shared calendar with color-coding by family member, recurring events, and reminders.

2. **Meal Planner:** Weekly meal planning with a recipe database. Drag recipes to days of the week.

3. **Shopping Lists:** Create and share grocery lists. Can generate lists from meal plans.

4. **Family Locator:** Real-time location sharing for family members (similar to Life360).

5. **Family Journal:** Shared photo album and journal for preserving family memories.

6. **Info Locker:** Store important documents, insurance info, emergency contacts.

7. **To-Do Lists:** Basic task lists with assignments and due dates.

### Picniic's Pricing

| Plan | Cost | Features |
|------|------|----------|
| **Free** | $0 | 2 family members, basic features |
| **Premium** | $7.99/mo | Unlimited members, all features |
| **Annual** | ~$60/yr (varies) | Same as premium, discounted |

**Important limitation:** Picniic's free tier is severely restricted to just 2 family members—essentially useless for the large families it targets.
