## Tips for Sustainable Meal Planning

### 1. Start with 3 Days, Not 7

Don't over-plan:
- Plan Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday
- See how it goes
- Add more days when comfortable

### 2. Have 5-10 "Default" Meals

Meals you can always make:
- Ingredients common
- Family likes them
- Quick to prepare
- Rotate these with new experiments

### 3. Theme Nights (Optional)

Reduces decisions:
- Taco Tuesday
- Pasta Wednesday
- Pizza Friday
- Kids cook Saturday

### 4. Account for Schedule

Don't plan elaborate dinner on:
- Late activity nights
- Both parents working late
- After-school chaos days

Plan slow cooker or quick meals accordingly.

### 5. Embrace Leftovers

Plan for leftovers:
- Sunday roast â†’ Monday sandwiches
- Taco meat â†’ Tuesday taco salad
- Less cooking, less waste
