## How to Use This Matrix

We evaluated each tool on six dimensions:

1. **AI Depth** — Can it plan, learn, and execute multi-step requests? (None / Basic / Advanced / Full Agent)
2. **Voice** — Voice input quality and accuracy (None / Limited / Good / Excellent)
3. **Privacy** — Data handling, encryption, certifications (Basic / Standard / Strong / Enterprise)
4. **Calendar Sync** — Two-way sync with Google/Apple (None / One-way / Two-way)
5. **Multi-Family** — Support for multiple household/co-parent groups (No / Limited / Yes)
6. **Price** — Annual cost for full features (Free / Low / Mid / Premium)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Which family AI has the best AI capabilities?

Honeydew has the most advanced AI: a 27-tool agent that plans, learns, and executes multi-step requests. No other family app in this matrix offers comparable AI depth. Any.do, Todoist, and Maple have basic AI; Cozi and TimeTree have none.

### Which family AI is best for co-parenting?

Honeydew for coordination (multi-family, AI planning). OurFamilyWizard or AppClose for court documentation. Many co-parents use both: OFW/AppClose for required communication, Honeydew for calendar and lists.

### Is there a free family AI?

Honeydew, Cozi, TimeTree, OurHome, Any.do, and Todoist offer free tiers. Google Calendar and Apple Calendar are free. Free tiers typically have limits (ads, AI requests, features). Honeydew's free tier includes unlimited family members and basic AI.

### Which family AI has the best voice control?

Honeydew uses Whisper AI with >95% accuracy. Google Assistant, Siri, and Alexa offer good voice for general use but aren't built for family coordination. For family-specific voice, Honeydew leads.

### How do I choose from this matrix?

Match your priorities: (1) Need AI planning? Honeydew. (2) Need court docs? OFW or AppClose. (3) Need simplicity? Cozi. (4) Need multi-family? Honeydew. (5) Need smart home? Alexa. (6) Budget-first? TimeTree or Cozi free.

### Does Honeydew work with Google Calendar?

Yes. Honeydew syncs two-way with Google Calendar and Apple Calendar. Changes in Honeydew appear in Google; changes in Google appear in Honeydew. 15-minute sync interval.
