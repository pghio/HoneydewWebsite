## Migrating from Cozi

### What You Can Bring
- Calendar events (export as .ics, import elsewhere)
- Contact information (manual transfer)
- Lists (screenshot or manual copy)
- Recipes (manual copy)

### What You'll Need to Recreate
- Recurring events
- Family member assignments
- Custom categories

### Migration Tips
1. Export Cozi calendar to Google Calendar first
2. Set up new app with family members
3. Recreate most-used recurring events
4. Add shopping list items as you need them (don't migrate all at once)
5. Run both apps in parallel for 1-2 weeks before fully switching
